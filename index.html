Choosing a Dog

When choose a dog, it is always important to take into consideration the environment the dog will be living in, the amount of time you will be able to spend with the dog and the financial responsibilty of owning a dog. The environment a day will be living in can affect the the breed of dog you will want to choose from, for an example smaller dogs will need less room to run around than bigger dogs. Time is a huge facter to consider when choose a day, as some dogs may need more attention than other, however all dogs need to attention in one form or the other. Finally when adopting a dog, it is important to understand the basic needs of a dog, which include food, toys and visits to the vet. All these things can add up over time and therefore future thought needs to be made before getting a dog.
